Why This Keeps Happening

You can see the pattern, and you've likely tried to change things. You know where the old behaviors and responses lead, and you genuinely want to respond in a different way. Yet somehow, when you find yourself in the moment, it is like something takes over and suddenly you are right back in the same reaction, the same behavior, or the same situation again.

It's frustrating and confusing. Especially when you have the awareness and you can clearly see what is happening. A lot of people begin questioning themselves at this point because they "know better" intellectually, yet they still find themselves acting in ways they do not fully understand or cannot seem to stop.

When you begin working with the underlying root of the pattern, you are no longer trying to force yourself to change the behavior from the surface level. You begin working with the deeper, underlying experiences and conditioning that have been driving your behaviors and responses. This is where people often begin experiencing more meaningful and sustainable shifts in how they think, feel, respond, and move through their lives.

Meet the Founder

Linda Dieffenbach

Reiki Master Teacher  ·  Holistic Coach  ·  Trauma-Informed Healer  ·  Author  ·  Speaker

"Healing creates a ripple effect that extends beyond yourself. As you step into your own transformation, you open space for others to heal and grow."

Linda Dieffenbach is a seasoned holistic practitioner, Reiki Master Teacher, and trauma-informed coach with over 20 years of experience in the healing arts. As the founder of Wellness in Harmony, she has dedicated her life to empowering individuals to heal, grow, and reclaim their inner strength. Through energy healing, trauma-informed coaching, and intuitive guidance, Linda creates a safe and sacred space where clients feel deeply seen, supported, and nurtured on their healing journey.

Blending her background in social work, energy healing, and coaching, Linda specializes in helping clients break free from limiting patterns, restore energetic balance, and cultivate deep personal transformation. As a trauma survivor herself, Linda ensures every client feels safe, seen, and supported — because she deeply understands the importance of this.
